Easy Summary

QuizZum is a tool that helps students learn better. It takes your notes, like from lectures or PDFs, and makes them easy to understand summaries. You can choose if you want a super simple (easy), a little more detail (medium), or a full explanation (hard) summary. It also makes quizzes for you to test what you learned. If you get something wrong, it helps you study that part again. The main goal is to help you learn everything without feeling stressed or overwhelmed.
